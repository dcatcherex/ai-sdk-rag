# Source Register for Thai Crop Pest and Disease Skill

Use this file to judge source quality and update references.

## Source priority

1. Thai Department of Agriculture (DOA): pesticide legality, registration, plant protection research, crop production standards.
2. Department of Agricultural Extension (DOAE): farmer-facing extension, outbreak warnings, pest calendars, safe pest management, local extension channels.
3. Thai Rice Department, universities, and commodity institutes: crop-specific biology and management details.
4. International sources such as FAO, IRRI, CABI, or university extension: symptom comparison and biology only. Do not use them to override Thai pesticide legality.

## Update checklist

When updating any crop or pesticide reference:

- Record the source URL and checked date.
- Prefer Thai official crop guidance over general internet summaries.
- Remove or mark any chemical as "verify label/current registration" if status is uncertain.
- Remove recommendations for prohibited substances immediately.
- Avoid dosage, mixing, and pre-harvest interval values unless tied to a current official label/source.
- Keep Thai farmer wording short and practical.
