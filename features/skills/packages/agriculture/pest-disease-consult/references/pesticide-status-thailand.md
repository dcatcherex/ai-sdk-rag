# Thailand Pesticide Status and Chemical Advice Guardrails

This file is a safety gate. Read it before recommending any chemical control in Thailand.

## Core rule

Do not treat any active ingredient as legal or crop-registered unless the current Thai label or Department of Agriculture (DOA) registration source supports that use.

When the reference material is not current enough to verify registration for the crop and pest/disease, say:

> ใช้เฉพาะสารที่ขึ้นทะเบียนกับพืชและศัตรูพืชนี้ อ่านฉลากก่อนใช้ และปรึกษาเจ้าหน้าที่เกษตรในพื้นที่

## Known prohibited / do not recommend

| Active ingredient | Thai name | Status for advice | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| chlorpyrifos | คลอร์ไพริฟอส | Do not recommend | DOA public material describes chlorpyrifos as hazardous substance type 4 and prohibited to produce, import, export, possess, or sell. |
| paraquat | พาราควอต | Do not recommend | DOA public material describes paraquat as hazardous substance type 4 and prohibited to produce, import, export, possess, or sell. |

If a farmer says they already have these substances:

- Do not give use instructions.
- Tell them not to use, sell, share, or store casually.
- Advise contacting the local DOA/DOAE office for lawful handling/disposal.

## Chemical advice hierarchy

Use this order:

1. Non-chemical field actions: remove infected material, improve drainage, prune for airflow, use clean planting material, crop rotation, sanitation, traps, bagging fruit, monitoring.
2. Biological or lower-risk options when supported by the crop/pest context.
3. Chemical class or active ingredient only when the reference supports it and it is not prohibited.
4. Always include label/PPE/harvest-interval caution.

## Never provide

- Brand names.
- Mixing rates unless copied from a current official label or official Thai guidance.
- Advice to use prohibited substances.
- Advice to mix multiple pesticides unless the label or official source says it is allowed.
- Advice to spray during wind, rain, high heat, flowering when pollinators are active, or close to harvest without label confirmation.

## Required chemical safety sentence

For Thai answers involving chemicals, include:

อ่านฉลากก่อนใช้ทุกครั้ง ใช้เฉพาะสารที่ขึ้นทะเบียนกับพืชและปัญหานี้ ใส่ถุงมือ หน้ากาก แว่นตา เสื้อแขนยาว และเว้นระยะเก็บเกี่ยวตามฉลาก

## Emergency chemical exposure

If the farmer reports dizziness, vomiting, breathing difficulty, skin/eye exposure, or accidental ingestion:

- Stop field advice.
- Tell them to call 1669 or go to hospital immediately.
- Tell them to bring the pesticide container or label if safe to do so.
