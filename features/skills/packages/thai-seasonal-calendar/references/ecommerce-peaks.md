## Platform-Specific Notes

**Shopee Thailand**
- Flash deals every hour during mega sales — content should push urgency
- Shopee Live streams spike during 11.11 and 12.12 — video content is highly effective
- Shopee Coins / vouchers are major decision factors — copy can reference "รับ Shopee Coin เพิ่ม"

**Lazada Thailand**
- LazMall brands get premium placements during mega sales
- "Grand Launch" and "Birthday Sale" (March) are Lazada-specific peaks
- Strong in electronics, beauty, and fashion categories

**LINE Shopping / LINE MyShop**
- LINE OA-native shopping — relevant for brands using Vaja's LINE integration
- LINE Friends collaborations spike during seasonal events
- Push notifications via LINE OA outperform email open rates in Thailand

**TikTok Shop Thailand**
- LIVE selling during 11.11/12.12 is massive — script live sessions with urgency + interaction
- "ช้อปผ่าน TikTok" (Shop via TikTok) gaining rapidly with younger audiences
- Trending sounds + product placement during peak seasons = high organic reach

## Content timing strategy

For mega sales, use this 3-phase structure:

**Phase 1 — Awareness (7–14 days before)**
- Tease products, build wishlist momentum
- "เพิ่มลงตะกร้าก่อนได้เลย" (add to cart now)
- Educational / benefit content

**Phase 2 — Urgency (2–3 days before)**
- Countdown timers, "เหลืออีกแค่ X วัน"
- Flash deal previews
- Stock warnings: "สินค้ามีจำนวนจำกัด"

**Phase 3 — Live day**
- "วันนี้เท่านั้น!" (today only)
- Live stream or story content every 2–3 hours
- Customer proof / UGC reposting
- CTA direct to product link
