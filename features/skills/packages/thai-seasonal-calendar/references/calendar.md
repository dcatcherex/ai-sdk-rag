# Thai Marketing Calendar — Month by Month

## January

| Date | Event | Type |
|------|-------|------|
| Jan 1 | New Year's Day (วันปีใหม่) | Public holiday |
| Jan (2nd Sat) | Children's Day (วันเด็กแห่งชาติ) | National day |
| Late Jan / Feb | Chinese New Year (ตรุษจีน) | Cultural (major for Thai-Chinese community) |

**Marketing angles:**
- **New Year (Jan 1):** Resolution season — "เริ่มต้นปีใหม่" messaging. Works well for health, education, productivity, home refresh. Gift sets and lucky promotions.
- **Children's Day:** Family activities, kids' products, educational content. Brands can do "เพื่อลูกน้อย" angles.
- **Chinese New Year:** Red/gold visuals, prosperity messaging ("ซินเจียยู่อี่"), gift box promotions, lucky number pricing (e.g., ฿888). Strong for food, jewelry, lifestyle, beauty.

---

## February

| Date | Event | Type |
|------|-------|------|
| Feb 14 | Valentine's Day | Commercial / popular |
| ~Feb (lunar) | Makha Bucha (มาฆบูชา) | Buddhist holiday (no alcohol sales) |

**Marketing angles:**
- **Valentine's:** Couples + self-love + friendships ("Galentine's"). Works across almost all categories. Thai market responds to both romantic and friendship angles. Include couple pricing, gift wrapping, limited editions.
- **Makha Bucha:** Mindful, merit-based content. Good for wellness, spiritual products, community/CSR messaging. Avoid alcohol promotions on this day.

---

## March

| Date | Event | Type |
|------|-------|------|
| Mar 8 | International Women's Day | Global / awareness |
| Mar–Apr | Pre-Songkran warm-up | Seasonal lead-up |

**Marketing angles:**
- **Women's Day:** Empower, celebrate, feature female founders/customers. Works well for beauty, fashion, wellness, education.
- **Pre-Songkran:** Start water/summer/travel themes 2–3 weeks before April 13. Sunscreen, swimwear, travel gear, snacks for road trips.

---

## April

| Date | Event | Type |
|------|-------|------|
| Apr 6 | Chakri Day (วันจักรี) | Public holiday |
| Apr 7 | World Health Day | Global |
| Apr 13–15 | Songkran (สงกรานต์) | Biggest Thai holiday |
| Apr 13 | Thai New Year (วันปีใหม่ไทย) | National |

**Marketing angles:**
- **Songkran (peak season):** Water, summer, family, travel, cultural pride. Biggest shopping and gifting moment of the year for many categories.
  - Water activity gear, cooling products, skincare (post-sun care), beverages
  - "สุขสันต์วันสงกรานต์" — festive blessing copy
  - "กลับบ้านปีใหม่ไทย" — homecoming / travel angle
  - Promote digital gifting for those who can't travel home
  - Limited-edition Songkran packaging or designs perform well
- **Health Day:** Wellness check-ups, health products, "สุขภาพดีเริ่มต้นที่นี่" positioning.

---

## May

| Date | Event | Type |
|------|-------|------|
| May 1 | Labour Day (วันแรงงาน) | Public holiday |
| May 4 | Coronation Day (วันฉัตรมงคล) | Public holiday — royal occasion |
| ~May (lunar) | Visakha Bucha (วิสาขบูชา) | Buddhist holiday (no alcohol) |
| May (variable) | Royal Ploughing Ceremony | Cultural |

**Marketing angles:**
- **Labour Day:** Worker appreciation, SME support angle, "ของขวัญให้ทีม" (gifts for your team).
- **Coronation Day:** Respectful, patriotic tone. Avoid hard selling.
- **Visakha Bucha:** Merit/giving angle. Wellness, spiritual, community CSR messaging.
- **School year begins (mid-May):** Back-to-school is a major spending peak — stationery, bags, uniforms, tutoring, tech for kids.

---

## June

| Date | Event | Type |
|------|-------|------|
| Jun (varies) | Mid-Year Sale season | E-commerce / retail |
| Jun 5 | World Environment Day | Global awareness |

**Marketing angles:**
- **Mid-Year Sale:** Price-led promotions, clearance, bundle deals. Major moment for Shopee/Lazada/LINE Shopping.
- **Environment Day:** Sustainable products, eco packaging, brand values content.

---

## July

| Date | Event | Type |
|------|-------|------|
| ~Jul (lunar) | Asahna Bucha (อาสาฬหบูชา) | Buddhist holiday (no alcohol) |
| Day after Asahna | Khao Phansa begins (เข้าพรรษา) | Buddhist Lent — 3-month period |

**Marketing angles:**
- **Khao Phansa (Buddhist Lent, ~Jul–Oct):** Traditional period for making merit, giving robes/offerings. Alcohol brands go quiet. Strong for wellness, temple-adjacent products, health foods, meditation/mindfulness.
- Gifting "เทียนพรรษา" (Buddhist lent candles) is a niche but real gifting occasion.

---

## August

| Date | Event | Type |
|------|-------|------|
| Aug 12 | Mother's Day / Queen's Birthday (วันแม่แห่งชาติ) | Major national holiday |
| Aug 12 | National holiday | Public |

**Marketing angles:**
- **Mother's Day (Aug 12):** One of the top gifting occasions in Thailand. Jasmine flowers (ดอกมะลิ) are the symbol.
  - Gift sets, flowers, jewelry, spa experiences, food, beauty
  - "รักแม่" (love mom) messaging — emotional storytelling is key
  - Campaigns work best 1–2 weeks before, peaking on Aug 12
  - User-generated content ("แชร์รูปกับแม่") performs well on Facebook/Instagram

---

## September–October

| Date | Event | Type |
|------|-------|------|
| ~Oct (lunar) | Ok Phansa (ออกพรรษา) | End of Buddhist Lent |
| ~Oct (lunar) | Vegetarian Festival (เทศกาลกินเจ) | Cultural (Chinese-Thai, 9 days) |
| Oct 13 | Memorial Day (วันคล้ายวันสวรรคต ร.9) | Solemn national day |
| Oct 23 | Chulalongkorn Day (วันปิยมหาราช) | Public holiday |

**Marketing angles:**
- **Vegetarian Festival (กินเจ):** Huge for food brands — vegan/vegetarian product lines, "เจ" labeling, plant-based alternatives. 9-day window is intense.
- **Ok Phansa:** End of Lent = merit-giving season (ทอดกฐิน / ทอดผ้าป่า). Also signals festive season beginning.
- **Chulalongkorn Day (Oct 23):** Respectful memorial tone. Not a sales day — brand awareness and values content works better.

---

## November

| Date | Event | Type |
|------|-------|------|
| Nov 11 | 11.11 Singles' Day | Major e-commerce peak |
| ~Nov (lunar) | Loy Krathong (ลอยกระทง) | Major cultural festival |
| Nov (variable) | Yi Peng Lantern Festival (เชียงใหม่) | Cultural / visual |

**Marketing angles:**
- **11.11:** Largest single-day sale event in SE Asia. Price-first content, countdown timers, flash deals, product bundles. All categories. Start warming up Nov 1.
- **Loy Krathong:** One of the most beautiful and romantic Thai holidays. Candlelit, water, full moon.
  - Romantic, dreamy content — couples, family, friends
  - "ขอพรลอยกระทง" (make a wish while floating the krathong)
  - Works especially well for travel, food/dining, beauty, photography-friendly products
  - Rich visual content performs (Instagram, TikTok)
  - Great for handmade/artisan brands

---

## December

| Date | Event | Type |
|------|-------|------|
| Dec 5 | Father's Day / Late King's Birthday (วันพ่อแห่งชาติ) | Major national holiday |
| Dec 10 | Constitution Day (วันรัฐธรรมนูญ) | Public holiday |
| Dec 12 | 12.12 | E-commerce peak |
| Dec 25 | Christmas | Commercial (urban/international) |
| Dec 31 | New Year's Eve (วันสิ้นปี) | Celebration |

**Marketing angles:**
- **Father's Day (Dec 5):** Sacred lily (ดอกพุทธรักษา) is the symbol. Similar gifting energy to Mother's Day — jewelry, clothing, experience gifts, food.
- **12.12:** Year-end clearance energy. Bundle deals, "ของขวัญปีใหม่" (New Year gifts). Works as a bridge between Father's Day and Christmas.
- **Christmas:** Urban and international audience. Works well for imported goods, lifestyle, tech, F&B. Not a Thai public holiday but heavily commercial in malls and online.
- **New Year's Eve/Day:** Countdown parties, year-in-review content, "สวัสดีปีใหม่" blessing posts. Strong CTA: "ต้อนรับปีใหม่ด้วย [product]".

## Year-round recurring windows

| Window | When | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| Payday peak | 25th–1st of every month | Thais typically get paid end of month. Promotions on the 25th–31st see higher conversion. |
| Buddhist holy days (วันพระ) | Every ~7 days (lunar quarter) | Some Thais avoid alcohol purchases on these days. Mostly relevant for F&B. |
| School holiday peaks | Mar–May, Oct | Family purchases, travel, education products spike |
| Ramadan | Mar–Apr (varies) | Relevant in southern Thailand and Muslim community segments |
