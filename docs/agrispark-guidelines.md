# **AgriSpark Hackathon 2.0 - AI Edition**

## **AI for Smarter Farmer Advisory**

**Tagline:** Build AI solutions that turn data into better farming decisions. Helping farmers make smarter choices — from planting to harvest.

## **1. Overview (ภาพรวม)**

### **1.1 Background**

- **Goal:** To focus on AI-driven solutions for providing advice and decision-making support.
- **Objective:** Develop solutions that help farmers, service providers, and stakeholders in the agricultural industry make better, faster, and more sustainable decisions.
- **Core Principle:** Farm Smarter because every farming decision counts.

### **1.2 The Challenge (โจทย์ปัญหา)**

- **Core Question:** "How can AI help farmers and advisory systems make smarter decisions?"
- **Expectation:** Solutions that transform complex data into simple, actionable recommendations for farmers or agricultural extension workers.

### **Key Focus Areas:**

- **Planting Plan:** Real-time & conditions.
- **Pest & Disease:** Early detection & alerts.
- **Soil & Nutrition:** Optimize inputs & soil health.
- **Weather & Risk:** Forecast & advisories.
- **Yield & Costs:** Improve profitability.

## **2. Proposal Submission Guidelines**

**Deadline (Round 1):** April 8, 2026, at 23:59.

### **2.1 Challenge Fit (ความเหมาะสมกับโจทย์)**

- **Origin:** Explain the background of the idea (e.g., personal experience, farmer interviews, workshops).
- **Target Audience:** Specify who will use the solution (e.g., smallholder farmers, extension officers, agri-business owners).
- **Focus Topic:**
- Crop Management
- Risk Mitigation
- Market Decisions
- Others
- **Current Challenges:** Describe the current pain points of the target group.
- **Solution Presentation:**

### **2.2 AI & Technical Approach**

- **AI Integration:**

- **Data Inputs:** Sources used (e.g., Weather, Soil, Satellite, Farmer data).
